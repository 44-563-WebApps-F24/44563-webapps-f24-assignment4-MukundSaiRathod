Mukund Sai presents a Greek play by Sophocles

Oedipus at Colonos

-Sophocles

THE :

Athenian citizens, my briefest tale Were to say singly, Oedipus is gone; But to describe the scene enacted yonder Craves no brief speech, nor was the action brief.

OED :

Then he is gone! Poor man!

MESS :

Know it once for all, He hath left eternally the light of day
